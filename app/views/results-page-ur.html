Skilled Worker visa If you’re coming to work in an eligible skilled job

Many types of job are eligible, including:

  • jobs that require a university degree, such as scientists or doctors
  • many managerial or skilled administrative roles
  • skilled trades, such as carpenters or plumbers

Service Supplier visa If you’re temporarily providing an eligible service in the UK

You may be able to provide services to a UK business.

The eligibility criteria include:

  • providing a service to a UK business under a contract that is covered by a valid international trade agreement
  • doing an eligible job - if your job is not eligible you may still qualify if you have relevant qualifications and experience
  • having worked for your employer for at least 12 months outside the UK, or have at least 12 months’ professional experience if you’re a self-employed service provider
