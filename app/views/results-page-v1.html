If you’re coming to work in an eligible skilled job

Skilled worker visa

Many types of job are eligible, including jobs that require a university degree, such as scientists or doctors, many managerial or skilled administrative roles, or skilled trades, such as carpenters or plumbers

Eligibility

  • working in an eligible job
  • having a job offer
  • being paid a minimum salary - how much depends on the type of work you do
  • proving that you can read, write, speak and understand English

How long you can stay

5 years

You can extend it as long as you’re still eligible.

After 5 years, you may be able to apply to settle permanently in the UK.
